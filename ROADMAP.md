\# White Brick NOC OS Roadmap



\## Vision



White Brick NOC OS is a cinematic Network Operations Center designed to monitor the Honeycutt Home Network and the future White Brick ecosystem.



The long-term goal is to create an always-on infrastructure operating system capable of monitoring:



\- Internet

\- Network

\- White Brick Nodes

\- ESP Devices

\- NAS

\- Cameras

\- Weather

\- PC Health

\- Home Automation

\- Future AI Services
