\# White Brick NOC OS Architecture



The software is divided into five major systems.



\## Engine



Responsible for rendering, timing, scenes, animation and performance.



\---



\## Visuals



Responsible for:



\- Globe

\- Radar

\- Timeline

\- Weather

\- Camera Wall

\- Graphs



\---



\## Telemetry



Responsible for:



\- Network

\- Internet

\- PC

\- NAS

\- White Brick

\- ESP



\---



\## Operator



Responsible for:



\- Console

\- Alerts

\- Investigation

\- Replay



\---



\## Ecosystem



Responsible for:



\- White Brick

\- Garage OS

\- Studio

\- Firmware

\- Future integrations
